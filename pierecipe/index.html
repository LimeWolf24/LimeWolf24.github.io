Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

One 9-inch Pie

30 min- prep time

10 min - cook time

40 min - total

8 serings

Ingridients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 T all-purpose flour
  • 2 T cornstarch
  • 1/4 t salt
  • 1 1/2 C water
  • 2 lemons, squeezed and zested
  • 2 T butter
  • 4 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1 (9-inch) pie crust, baked
  • 4 egg whites
  • 6 T white sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
